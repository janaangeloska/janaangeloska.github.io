During this period, Jana studied several computer science disciplines, developing her programming talents and broadening her knowledge of the area.


As a dedicated student, Jana devotes countless hours to her coursework and actively participates in extracurricular activities related to her field of study. She consistently demonstrates a strong work ethic, determination, and a natural aptitude for problem-solving.
