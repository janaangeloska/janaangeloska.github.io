Meet Jana!

Jana Angeloska was born on July 9, 2003 in Ohrid, Macedonia. She is the daughter of Sasho Angeloski (born in Ohrid, Macedonia) and Biljana Angeloska (born in Skopje, Macedonia) and has a younger brother Nikola Angeloski(born in Ohrid, Macedonia).


For the first 18 years of her life, Jana resided in her hometown of Ohrid. It was a place that held significant importance to her, shaping her early years and providing a nurturing environment for her personal and academic growth. Ohrid not only served as the backdrop for her childhood memories but also played a crucial role in her every-day experiences. It was in Ohrid that Jana completed her elementary and high school education. She thrived academically, consistently demonstrating her intellectual prowess and a natural inclination for learning.


During her high school years, Jana led an exceptionally busy life. Despite the demanding schedule, she remained incredibly passionate and dedicated to her various endeavors. Notably, Jana developed a profound interest in handball, and became known for her prowess on the handball court and inspired those around her with her commitment, resilience, and team spirit.


Ohrid also observed Jana's musical career take off. Jana began her musical adventure in primary school and continued through high school, experimenting with numerous genres and honing her skills as a vocalist and guitarist. Her love of music became a source of joy and self-expression for her.


However, at a crossroads in her life, Jana decided to pursue higher study in the capital city of Skopje. She said goodbye to Ohrid and began a new chapter in her life with the objective of broadening her horizons and digging deeper into her academic interests and she keeps the spirit of Ohrid with her as she pursues her passions and goals.
